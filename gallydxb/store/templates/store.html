Our Story

Founded in 2000 in Calicut by four childhood friends with a shared love for fashion, Gally began as a retail store on SM Street. Their strong bond and fresh ideas quickly made waves in the local fashion scene. Inspired by travel and trends, they expanded into wholesale, leveraging direct ties with manufacturers in India and China. In 2008, they launched wholesale outlets in Calicut and Bangalore, each partner taking charge of key operations. By 2010, they set up their own production unit in Bangalore to support growing demand.

Seeking global reach, Gally entered the Dubai market in 2013, partnering with a local entrepreneur. With steady growth, they became a trusted name in men’s apparel wholesale across the Middle East by 2018. Today, Gally Dubai serves the GCC and Africa, sourcing from factories in India, China, and Turkey, and producing private label collections for global retailers.
