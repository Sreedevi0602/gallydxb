Gally’s Global Gateway: Dubai & the GCC

In 2013, Gally made its first international move by stepping into the bustling trade hub of Dubai. With a vision to explore new horizons, the founders teamed up with a local entrepreneur and opened their first wholesale store in the city. What began as a cautious entry into foreign territory quickly transformed into a major milestone. Dubai’s dynamic market and global connectivity became the perfect launchpad for Gally’s international expansion.

By 2018, Gally had earned a reputation as a trusted name in men’s fashion wholesale across the Middle East, thanks to its dedication to quality, trend awareness, and personalized service. Today, Gally Dubai is more than just a store—it’s the regional hub for distributing high-quality apparel. With its own production units in India, China, and Turkey, Gally Dubai also leads the brand’s private label manufacturing for global retail partners.

Gally India: The Root of Innovation

The heart of Gally lies in its roots—India. What began as a single store in Calicut has evolved into a comprehensive ecosystem of retail, wholesale, and manufacturing. With a deep understanding of Indian fashion sensibilities and a pulse on global trends, Gally India remains the brand's creative and production powerhouse.

From launching wholesale outlets in Calicut and Bangalore to establishing a dedicated production unit in Bangalore, Gally’s growth in India showcases the strength of strategic planning and teamwork. India continues to play a crucial role in sourcing, innovation, and design, fueling Gally’s global operations with quality and creativity at scale. Gally India isn’t just the beginning of the journey—it’s the engine that continues to power the brand forward.

Gally in Africa: Fashion Beyond Borders

Driven by the success in Dubai and the GCC, Gally expanded its reach to Africa, tapping into an exciting and fast-growing market. Leveraging its logistics and trade operations based in Dubai, Gally began supplying men’s fashion to major African cities and retail hubs.

Africa’s dynamic demand for quality and affordable fashion aligned perfectly with Gally’s core values. Through strong relationships with regional wholesalers and retailers, Gally has become a trusted name known for consistency, affordability, and trend-conscious collections. Today, Gally Africa symbolizes the brand’s adaptability and vision—to connect with diverse markets while maintaining the essence of quality and trust.
